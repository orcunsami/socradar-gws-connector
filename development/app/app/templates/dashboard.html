New here? How this connector works
  1. Configure the tenant in Settings: your Verified domains, the Feed window, and paste the SOCRadar feed key.
  2. Enable the actions your team is allowed to take (also in Settings). Enabling an action only makes its button available; nothing runs on its own.
  3. Run a scan (the button above). The connector reads the SOCRadar feed, keeps the emails inside your verified domains, and checks each against your Workspace directory. Matches show up in Flagged Users.
  4. Act on a flagged user: click an action (e.g. Revoke sessions). Every action is logged in Audit, in that user's Activity, and per Scan.

Remediation mode is manual by default (you click each action). Two-person approval and autonomous (auto-on-scan) modes are optional and off by default.
